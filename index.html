ThermoVolt Pro - Chiller | STM32

Designed and developed a multifunctional industrial-grade monitoring and control device featuring 4-digit 7-segment display, RTD-based temperature sensing, power monitoring, relay control, and analog 4-20mA output with user-configurable settings and hysteresis.

Deltz Robot | ROS2 | RPI

Developed a Deltz robot using Raspberry Pi and ESP32 with ROS2 for control, featuring 3D-printed TPU components, servo actuation, and init color, IR, and limit sensors for precise motion and feedback.
